Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Jack is a daily habit tracking and goal management app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve the Jack app experience.

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information: your email address and name, provided when you sign in via our OAuth authentication flow
  • Habit and goal data: the goals, habits, check-in ratings, and notes you create within the app
  • Vision board content: photos you choose to upload from your device's photo library
  • Team and community content: team names, messages, and posts you share with your team members
  • Alarm configuration: your preferred alarm time and days

Information collected automatically:

  • Device information: device type and operating system version, used for compatibility and bug diagnosis
  • App usage data: feature interactions and error logs, used to improve app stability (no personally identifiable information is included)

We do not collect precise location data, contacts, microphone audio, or any sensitive personal information beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to operate and improve the Jack app:

  • To sync your habits, check-ins, and goals across your devices
  • To enable team accountability features, including shared progress and team messaging
  • To schedule and deliver local alarm notifications on your device
  • To provide analytics and progress insights within the app
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance
We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Jack uses an offline-first architecture. Your habit data, check-ins, and settings are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and are also synced to our secure servers to enable cross-device access.

Our servers are hosted on infrastructure with industry-standard security practices, including encrypted connections (TLS/HTTPS) for all data in transit and encrypted storage for data at rest. Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized systems and personnel.

Vision board photos you upload are stored on secure cloud storage (S3-compatible) and are accessible only to your account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your team members: If you join a team, your daily check-in scores and shared goal progress are visible to other members of that team. You control which goals are shared.
  • Service providers: We use trusted third-party services (such as cloud hosting and authentication providers) that process data solely on our behalf and under strict data protection agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and habit data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Local data stored on your device (via AsyncStorage) is managed by your device's operating system and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all your habit, check-in, and goal data directly within the app at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete individual habits, goals, or check-in entries within the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at the email address below
  • Export: You may request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Opt out of notifications: You can disable alarm notifications at any time through the app's Settings screen or your device's system notification settings

7. Children's Privacy

Jack is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Third-Party Authentication

Jack uses an OAuth-based sign-in flow (email magic link). We receive only your email address and name from this process. We do not receive access to any other accounts or services linked to your email provider.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
